      Basic Duties

      You serve as a record keeper, an administrator, and a trainer to the other clerks and secretaries.

      Record Keeper

      As a record keeper, you need to keep accurate and current membership records, financial records, member progress lists and reports, stake leadership meeting minutes, and an annual stake history. You will also prepare boundary and leadership change proposals.

      Administrator

      As an administrator, you provide membership reports, financial reports, and other vital information to help the stake president and other stake leaders make important decisions. You analyze trends, strengths, and weaknesses in your stake.

      Trainer

      As a trainer, you also supervise the work of assistant stake clerks, stake secretaries, and ward clerks. You make sure they understand their record keeping responsibilities, and demonstrate how to ensure the confidentiality of records, and how to use church record keeping software. You will also impress upon them the value and importance of their work. Your calling is important. The Lord trusts you.
